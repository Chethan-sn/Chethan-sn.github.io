GOBBLE-TOE is just like regular tic-tac-toe with few enhancements

Enhancements:

  • Each player gets 1-6 levels of boxes
  • Each box can be used only once
  • One can gobble/replace other players box of lesser level
  • Its a win as soon as one gets 3 boxes in a row
  • Its a draw if the board fills up or the boxes are empty
  • You play first
Have fun !!
